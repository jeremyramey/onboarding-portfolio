## My Approach to Onboarding

My onboarding philosophy is centered on ownership, structure, and momentum. Every onboarding moment is an opportunity to either build confidence or introduce friction.

- Clear ownership from kickoff through go-live  
- Defined milestones and success criteria  
- Early enablement of admins and end users  
- A fast, deliberate path to customer independence  
- Strong alignment with Product, Engineering, and Customer Success
